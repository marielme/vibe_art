Interactive ML Art Gallery

Vibe Shift Hackathon - October 2025

A collection of interactive art experiments combining body movement, machine learning, and generative visuals. Each piece uses real-time pose detection to transform your movements into unique artistic expressions.

Experiment 1: Dots

Simple particle system that responds to your body movements with flowing dots and trails.

Launch Experience

Experiment 2: Voronoi

Geometric patterns and voronoi diagrams that connect and react to your pose keypoints.

Launch Experience

Experiment 3: Painting

Watercolor-inspired effects with paint splatters and flowing colors based on your acceleration.

Launch Experience

Experiment 4: Claude Logo Symphony

Logo particles, musical interaction, and skeleton visualization with Swan Lake theme variations.

Launch Experience

Created with p5.js, ml5.js, and Tone.js | All processing happens locally in your browser
